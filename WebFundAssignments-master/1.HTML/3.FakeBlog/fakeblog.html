F.R.I.E.N.D.S

F.R.I.E.N.D.S. Friends is an American television sitcom, created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004, lasting ten seasons.

F.R.I.E.N.D.S

  1. Rachel Green
  2. Monica Geller
  3. Ross Geller
  4. Phoebe Buffay
  5. Chandler Bing
  6. Joey Tribbiani

Some of the Awards for this TV show are:

  • Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Comedy Series
  • American Comedy Award
  • Golden globe Award
  • GLAAD media award
